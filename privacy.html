Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 | Last Updated: November 10, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At PillReminder, we believe your health information is deeply personal and should remain private. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

The short version: In version 1.0, we don't collect any personal data. Your medication information stays on your device.

1. Information We Don't Collect

PillReminder version 1.0 is designed with privacy at its core. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your medication names, dosages, or schedules
  • Your health information or medical history
  • Your personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your location data
  • Your emergency contacts information
  • Your usage patterns or medication adherence data
  • Any photos or images you may add to your medications
  • Any notes or comments you add to your medication entries

2. Where Your Data Lives

All your medication data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Local Storage Only: Your medication information, schedules, reminders, and all app data are stored locally on your iPhone using secure iOS storage mechanisms.
  • No Cloud Sync: Version 1.0 does not include cloud synchronization features. Your data never leaves your device.
  • No Servers: We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal information.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Since we don't collect your data, we cannot share it with third parties.

3. iOS System Integration

PillReminder uses standard iOS features to function properly:

  • Notifications: We use iOS notification services to deliver medication reminders. These notifications are generated locally on your device.
  • Contacts (Optional): If you choose to use the emergency contact feature, we request permission to access your contacts. Contact information is stored locally and only used to send alerts if you miss critical medications.
  • App Data Backup: Your medication data may be included in your iCloud or iTunes backups if you have device backups enabled. This is controlled by your iOS settings, not by PillReminder.

4. Emergency Contact Feature

If you enable the emergency contact notification feature:

  • Emergency contact information is stored locally on your device only.
  • When you miss a critical medication, the app may send a notification to your designated emergency contacts using iOS messaging capabilities.
  • These notifications are sent directly from your device using standard iOS messaging features.
  • No data passes through our servers during this process.
  • You have complete control over which contacts receive alerts and can modify or remove them at any time.

5. No Analytics or Tracking

Version 1.0 of PillReminder does not include:

  • Analytics tools or tracking software
  • Advertising networks or ad tracking
  • Third-party SDKs that collect user data
  • Crash reporting that includes personal information
  • Usage metrics or behavioral tracking

6. App Permissions

PillReminder may request the following iOS permissions:

  • Notifications: Required to send you medication reminders. All notifications are generated locally on your device.
  • Contacts (Optional): Only if you choose to set up emergency contacts. You can decline this permission and still use all other app features.

You can review and modify these permissions at any time in your iOS Settings under PillReminder.

7. Data Security

Even though your data never leaves your device, we take security seriously:

  • Your medication data is stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms.
  • We follow Apple's security best practices for iOS app development.
  • Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).
  • We recommend keeping your device secured and updated with the latest iOS version.

8. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app at any time.
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any medication, reminder, or contact information directly in the app.
  • Delete: You can delete all app data by uninstalling PillReminder from your device.
  • Export: Currently, there is no export feature, but since all data is local, it's included in your device backups.

9. Children's Privacy

PillReminder can be used by individuals of any age to manage their medications. However:

  • We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.
  • Parents or guardians may use the app to manage their children's medications.
  • All data remains on the device and is controlled by the device owner.

10. Future Versions and Feature Updates

We are committed to maintaining your privacy as we develop PillReminder. If future versions include new features that affect privacy, we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy with clear explanations of what has changed.
  • Notify you within the app about significant privacy-related changes.
  • Request your explicit consent before enabling any features that collect or share data.
  • Provide clear opt-in choices for any optional data collection.

Planned Features (Future Versions):

  • Cloud Sync: If implemented, will be entirely optional and require your explicit consent.
  • Caretaker Access: Will use secure, encrypted connections and require your authorization.
  • Usage Analytics: If added, will be anonymous and aggregated, with the ability to opt out.

11. Third-Party Services

PillReminder version 1.0 does not integrate with any third-party services. We do not use:

  • Third-party analytics platforms
  • Social media integrations
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Payment processors (beyond Apple's App Store for subscriptions)

12. App Store and Payments

If you purchase a subscription or premium features:

  • All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store.
  • We do not receive or store your payment information.
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing.
  • We may receive anonymous transaction confirmations from Apple to enable premium features, but these do not include personal or payment information.

13. International Users

PillReminder works entirely on your device regardless of where you are located:

  • Since no data is collected or transmitted to servers, there are no international data transfers.
  • Your data remains on your device subject to the laws of your jurisdiction.
  • The app complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations by design (privacy by default).

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The updated policy will be posted within the app and on our website.
  • The version number and "Last Updated" date will be revised.
  • For significant changes that affect data collection practices, we will notify you through the app.
  • You can always find the current version in the app settings or at our website.

15. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how PillReminder handles your data, please contact us:

  • Developer: Saltuk Alakus
